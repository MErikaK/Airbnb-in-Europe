# Holiday Guide: Airbnb Across Europe 

## PROJECT OVERVIEW
This project analyzes prices, reviews, occupancy, and amenities of more than 95,000 Airbnb listings across 36 European countries and 373 cities. It focuses on price differences and examines additional fees and the availability of pet-friendly accommodations across different countries.

## DATA SOURCES
•	Kaggle dataset: Airbnb Market Data: Europe 373 Cities

Downloaded on 15/04/2026; Last update of the dataset: 18/03/202

## DATA PREPARATION & LIMITATIONS
**Scope of Data:**
The dataset contains more than 95,000 Airbnb listings. It represents a large portion of the European Airbnb market, but not all European countries are included. It does not contain information about any other accommodation type besides Airbnb listings.

**Data Quality Considerations:**
Although the dataset is not fully complete, the proportion of missing data is very low. In key categories such as price and location, there is no missing data.
The accommodation price represents the average nightly rate per listing, not per person. The price data reflect the trailing twelve months (TTM) prior to March 2026.

**Feature Engineering:**
The location data required extensive cleaning due to transcription inconsistencies between local and international alphabets. ISO 3 country codes were added to improve geographical visualization.
The extensive amenities section was reduced to six key amenities: Wi-Fi, free parking, grill/fire pit, pool, piano and pet-friendliness.

## DASHBOARD STRUCTURE
The report is structured into two analytical sections:
1.	Introduction and Prices – TOP expensive and budget-friendly destinations, additional fees
2.	Reviews and Amenities  – Review ratings, amenities, and occupancy 

## KEY FINDINGS
- Location is the strongest predictor of price. 
- There is almost no difference in average rating of AirBnB accommodations across countries. 
- The share of pet-friendly Airbnb accommodations varies significantly among countries.
